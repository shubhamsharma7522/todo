Our Story & Vision

Travel Baadsha is a premium family-owned travel and rental venture that has proudly served corporate entities, families, and leisure travelers across India for more than a decade. Our reputation is built on absolute punctuality, flawless safety standards, and transparent support.

We believe that a journey should be as pleasant as the destination itself. That is why we provide immaculate, modern vehicles piloted by handpicked, verified professional chauffeurs.

Elite Team of Experts

Our dedicated operations managers and drivers hold decades of collective route navigation and highway safety experience.

Customer-First Support

We boast a 94% customer retention rate by providing rapid response channels and transparent booking structures.

Unyielding Safety Standards

Every single vehicle in our sedan, SUV, and coach fleet undergoes rigorous regular fitness and sanitation inspections.

Modern Fleet Options

From luxury executive sedans and rugged family SUVs to spacious group tourist vans and luxury buses, we cover all travel requirements.
